## OurFamilyWizard

OurFamilyWizard has four plan tiers: Basic ($9.17/month), Essentials ($12.50/month), Premium ($18/month), and Max ($24.99/month), all priced per parent, billed annually. Monthly billing is not prominently listed on the pricing page.

The Basic tier covers the core platform. Essentials adds calling (45 minutes/month), a writing assistant, GPS check-ins, and expense payment features. Premium adds unlimited calling and unlimited certified record storage. Max adds call recordings and transcripts.

Third-party accounts for lawyers, therapists, and other professionals are free. Child accounts are free. The platform has a fee waiver program. Pricing is per individual parent — both parents need separate subscriptions, though one parent can pay for the other.

The platform's certified records and professional access features are frequently cited in discussions of court-ready documentation. What "certified" means in a specific legal proceeding remains a question for a lawyer familiar with the case.

*Pricing source: ourfamilywizard.com/pricing, May 2026.*

## AppClose

AppClose has a single paid tier at approximately $8.99/month (mobile) or $7.99/month (web), billed monthly per parent. There is no annual-only requirement. The platform offers a 60-day free trial with no credit card required.

All features are included at one price: secure messaging, encrypted calls and video, shared calendar, expense tracking and payments, unlimited certified business records, unlimited file storage, and a solo mode that allows one parent to use the platform for record-keeping even without the co-parent's participation.

A free tier is available for people experiencing financial hardship and domestic violence survivors, by application.

*Pricing source: appclose.com/pricing, May 2026.*

## 2houses

2houses uses a per-family pricing model: one subscription covers both parents, children, and authorized third parties (including mediators). The price is approximately $14.17/month, billed as $169.99 annually. A 14-day free trial is available.

The platform includes a shared calendar, expense management, secure messaging, a family journal for sharing photos and updates, and an information bank. Its per-family pricing structure means the cost is not doubled for the second parent, which distinguishes it from the per-parent model.

*Pricing source: 2houses.com/en/pricing, May 2026.*

---

## TalkingParents

TalkingParents has three tiers: Essentials ($7/month), Enhanced ($16/month), and Ultimate ($32/month), all priced per parent. Annual billing reduces cost by approximately 8%. A 30-day free trial is available.

Essentials includes secure messaging, shared calendar, an information library, 30 minutes of accountable calling, expense tracking, and 1 GB storage. Enhanced adds 60-minute calling, 15 GB storage, and unlimited PDF records. Ultimate adds unlimited recorded calls, a printed records package, a sentiment scanner with writing assistance, 50 GB storage, and priority support.

Fee waivers are available for qualifying users.

*Pricing source: talkingparents.com/pricing, May 2026.*
